PTBO Game Jam 01

Our inaugural event was held August 13 to 14, 2016 at the Holiday Inn Peterborough-Waterfront. It was an astounding success, drawing more than 40 visitors to the venue, with 17 developers participating as jammers over the weekend. With 78 slices of pizza being devoured by jammers whilst working on 11 games, based on our controversial Cats On Leashes theme.

A special thanks to dotBunny, Epic Games, Unity, Igniter Tickets, GitHub, Funky Rustic, and ardeeXYZ for their invaluable support of this event.
