PTBO Game Jam 02

Our second event was held February 17 to 19, 2017 at Fleming College’s Kawartha Trades and Technology Centre. It was a resounding success, drawing more than 150 visitors to the venue, with 81 developers participating as jammers over the weekend. With 516 slices of pizza being devoured by jammers whilst working on 33 games, based on our #AlternativeFacts theme, no developer went hungry. The Most Valuable Jammer award went to Arin Blue this event, for his dedication to helping his fellow jammers.

A special thanks to dotBunny (yay us?), Fleming College, Funky Rustic, GitHub, IBM, Peterborough Tech Advisory Council, Ricky’s All Day Grill, acorn30, Aramark, Audiokinetic, Community Futures Peterborough, Gotta Havva Pizza, inXile Entertainment, Kawartha Lakes Community Futures Development Corporation, Liftlock Studios, Pagefolio and Unity for their invaluable support of this event.
