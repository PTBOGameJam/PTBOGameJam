Sometimes it is a good idea to layout a rough plan of development; so thats exactly what we did for the PTBO Game Jam 03.

  • 4 Aug

  • 5 Aug

  • 6 Aug

5:30 PM
Early Registration

Drop by and sign in and register, deal with paperwork, and get your badge, making your life (and ours) easier Saturday morning.

5:30 PM - 7:30 PM
Youth Educational Block: Scratch

You have got to start somewhere; and there is no better place to start making games then with Briar teaching a great foundation in Scratch.

6:00 PM - 8:30 PM
Adult Education Block: How to make _____?

Matthew Davey and Robert French lead a 3 hour mini-instructional jam about the steps and overall process involved in making a game in Unity and Unreal.

8:00 AM
Doors Open

The doors open on PTBO Game Jam 03, the start of another fun filled adventure through time and space. .

8:45 AM
PTBO Game Jam 03 Intro

Matthew takes to the stage to welcome everyone to the third PTBO Game Jam.

9:00 AM
The Clock Starts

The 32 hour timer begins.

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
Pastries with Prima IP

Ever had a question about Intellectual Property? Patents? How about Trademarks? Why not have a delicious pastry and listen to what Marcelo Sarkis from Prima IP has to say!

5:00 PM
Done! Let the Rest & Relaxation Begin!

At this point we all will most likely need a bit of a mental break, and depending if you were here the entire time a shower.

5:00 PM - 6:00 PM
Show & Tell

Time to see what everyone else was doing while you were hyper focused on your own game.

6:00 PM
Wrap Up & Goodbyes

You don't have to go home, but you can't stay here. Matthew Davey will give his closing remarks and the work on the next PTBO Game Jam will begin.
