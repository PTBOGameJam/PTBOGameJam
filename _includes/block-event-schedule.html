SCHEDULE

Sometimes it is a good idea to layout a rough plan of development; so thats exactly what we did for the PTBO Game Jam 02.

  • 17 Feb
    Day 1
  • 18 Feb
    Day 2
  • 19 Feb
    Day 3
6:00 PM
On-Site Sign In Begins

Come and signin, get your pass and room assignment as well as your swag bag. You've now got some time to setup, explore the venue and orientate yourself with what could be your home for the next 48 hours.

6:00 PM - 8:30 PM
Game Development Seminar: How to make Pac-Man

Robert French leads a 2.5 hour mini-instructional jam about the steps and overall process involved in making Pac-Man.

8:30 PM - 8:40 PM
Introduction to PTBO Game Jam 02

Our Founder & Organzier, Matthew Davey takes to the stage to welcome participants and observers to the second PTBO Game Jam, introductions to the team that made this event possible, as well as praise all of the outstanding sponsors.

8:40 PM - 8:45 PM
Fleming College Supports Innovation

Maxine Mann, Dean of the School of Trades & Technology at Fleming College welcomes the PTBO Game Jam to the Kawartha Trades & Technology Centre.

8:45 PM - 8:55 PM
Watson Wants To Help

Angela Hopewell from IBM Canada talks about the role IBM can play in the game development process, and what Watson can do for developers.

9:00 PM
Let the Game Making Begin

It is time to start the quest, the adventure, the eye opening experience, the 45 hour grind to find ones creation.

10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Tim Hortons Is Open

As well as providing some coffee for participants during the event, Aramark has done everyone a solid and is opening up the Tim Horton's restaurant at the venue for this period on Satudray. Incase of stampede, remember, elbows up.

7:30 PM
Halfway Point

Is your game working? Do you have something slightly playable? Is it fun? How can you refine it more. All good questions to be asking yourself at this point.

11:00 PM
Late Night Pizza Party

Pagefolio & acorn30 show the local love and are planning on having Pizza brought in for participants for a little late night nurishment. Pepperoni, cheese and double anchovies. Just kidding, no anchovies. There may be more toppings, who knows!

8:00 AM - 10:00 AM
#GameDev Breakfast

The wonderful staff of Ricky's All Day Grill would like to support the hardworking Game Developers by preparing a wonderful breakfast for them.

6:00 PM
Done! Let the Rest & Relaxation Begin!

At this point we all will most likely need a bit of a mental break, and depending if you were here the entire time a shower.

6:15 PM - 6:30 PM
Supporting Innovation & Local Industry

Maryam Monsef, the Member of Parliament for Peterborough-Kawartha, and the Minister of Status of Women speaks to the importance of supporting events like the PTBO Game Jam.

6:30 PM - 7:30 PM
Show & Tell

Time to see what everyone else was doing while you were hyper focused on your own game. We are encouraging developers to show off their games in the presentation room (01). Just make sure you've got HDMI out and you should be good! Maybe even VGA, but you will be harassed about that. Media will be encouraged to be present at this portion of the event.

7:30 PM
Wrap Up & Goodbyes

You don't have to go home, but you can't stay here. Matthew Davey will give his closing presentation and the work on the next PTBO Game Jam will begin.
