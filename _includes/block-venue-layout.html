VENUE LAYOUT

With over 87,000 square feet of space, the Kawartha Trade & Technology Centre stands as a beacon of technological innovation in Peterborough, Ontario providing orginizations like ours with a fantastic environment to foster industry growth and development.

Parking

Parking is available across all lots on the Fleming College campus, just make sure to print a copy of your ticket to leave on your dash as it will serve as a parking pass. Otherwise its $12 per day, so plan ahead and print the ticket.

Registration

Both visitor and participants must first sign in at this location before proceeding into the event space. Any person found without a valid pass will be asked to sign in or leave the premises immediately.

Food & Catering

Throughout the course of the event some sponsors may choose to bring in food and/or drinks for participants, we will make those available in the designated location. Outside of Saturday morning (7am - 11am), participants are permitted to order food to the event so long as they receive it at the registration table.

Workspaces

On the map we've indicated the five primary work rooms that we will have setup and available for participants, this however does not preclude participants from using other breakout areas found throughout the KTTC.
