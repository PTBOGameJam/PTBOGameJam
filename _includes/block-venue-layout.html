Parking

Parking is FREE in the Spruce, Birch, Pine and Cedar lots (map) after 5PM on Friday. Please be aware that the bank of electric charge stations in the Spruce lot (out front of the KTTC) are reserved for staff parking only.

Registration

Both visitors and participants are required to sign in inside of room #1. Any person found without a valid pass will be asked to sign in or leave the premises immediately.

Food & Catering

Throughout the course of the event you may choose to bring in food and/or drinks, we would prefer you try to keep the mess to the lounge area. However, food and drinks are allowed in the main area.
