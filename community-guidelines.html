By participating in any PTBO Game Jam events (including the PTBO Game Dev Meetup), and/or communicating through the associated Discord server, you accept the following terms and conditions (the "community guidelines") in addition to any pre-existing terms and conditions.

Don't Be A Jerk

Everyone is equal, don’t upset that balance. Treat each other with respect, and it will be given in return.

Don't Be Fake

We are a community built to foster and support growth. Be legit.

Don't Spam

Paid advertising opportunities are reserved for sponsors of our events. It’s how we get all that phat loot for you.

If You See Something, Say Something

Sometimes things go sideways, just let us know.

Violation of any of the above guidelines may result in any or all of the following without warning: a temporary ban from the Discord server, a permanent ban from the Discord server, removal from an event, and/or a permanent ban from attending events.
