PTBO Game Jam 02

Our second event was held February 17 to 19, 2017 at Fleming College’s Kawartha Trades and Technology Centre. It was a resounding success, drawing more than 150 visitors to the venue, with 81 developers participating as jammers over the weekend. With 516 slices of pizza being devoured by jammers whilst working on 33 games, based on our #AlternativeFacts theme, no developer went hungry.

A special thanks to dotBunny (yay us?), Fleming College, Funky Rustic, GitHub, IBM, Peterborough Tech Advisory Council, Ricky’s All Day Grill, acorn30, Aramark, Audiokinetic, Community Futures Peterborough, Gotta Havva Pizza, inXile Entertainment, Kawartha Lakes Community Futures Development Corporation, Liftlock Studios, Pagefolio and Unity for their invaluable support of this event.

Games

An impressive 33 games were created throughout PTBO Game Jam 02, themed according to #AlternativeFacts. The theme definately presented it's own unique challenges in application, but jammers were still able to create a wide variety of titles.
